Fleur

Fleur is a great project made for a flower shop. The main task was to create the visual identity of the brand (including logo, menu, visual elements), some merchandising elements and a website. The design of the project was inspired by the style of the Victorian era, characterized by an intense elegance and seriousness. In particular, inspiration comes from different fields: architecture, fashion, graphics, interior design elements, typical sketches of insects and animals of that period.

brand identity
logo design
graphic design

The dragonfly was taken as a reference point, present in fascinating graphic representations and sketches, for its particular meaning of freedom, peace and awareness. In earlier times this elegant insect was seen as a magical being. On the other side, the dragonfly it is seen as a symbol of the search for one's own identity and the affirmation of personality. These are the characteristics that we want to communicate to Fleur's customers: it is a space of freedom and inner peace, almost a place for meditation. Flowers can inspire us and guide us to conquer the new.
